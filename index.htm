Class Homepage
This is your class homepage. To submit an assignment, click on the "Submit" button to the right of the assignment name. If the Submit button is grayed out, no submissions can be made to the assignment. If resubmissions are allowed, the submit button will read "Resubmit" after you make your first submission to the assignment. To view the report you have submitted, click the "View" button. Once the assignment's post date has passed, you will also be able to view the feedback left on your paper by clicking the "View" button.
